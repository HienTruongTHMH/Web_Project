Strawberry Cream Cheesecake

👁️ 65k 🕒 Yesterday ❤️ 25 ★★★★★

One thing I learned when living in Canarsie section of Brooklyn, NY was how to cook a good Italian meal. Here is a recipe I created after having this dish in a restaurant. Hope you enjoy it!

Prep Time

15 MIN

Servings

4 People

Ingredients

For the crust

  • 400g graham crackers
  • 150g unsalted butters, melted

For the cheesecake

  • 300g marshmallows
  • 500g Philadelphia cream cheese, softened
  • 250ml thickened/whipping cream, warm
  • 3 tbsp powdered gelatin + 3 tbsp water
  • 5 drops purple food gel
  • 3 drops blue food gel

Instructions

  • To prepare crust add graham crackers to a food processor and pulse. Add melted butter and pulse 3–4 times.

  • Pour mixture into a 20cm (8") tart tin. Use a spoon to press. Chill for 30 min.

  • Microwave marshmallows and butter for 30s. Mix to combine.

  • Combine gelatin and water. Microwave 30s. Mix to combine.

  • Add cream cheese and beat until smooth.

  • Add warm cream and gelatin. Mix well.

  • Split into bowls. Add food gel to 1/3. Leave the rest white.

  • Pour into tart. Swirl with skewer. Chill for 2 hours.

  • Slice with a knife to serve.
